Project: Design and Development of a Fruit-Sorting Machine

I designed and developed a complete fruit-sorting machine from scratch as part of my mechanical engineering work. The goal was to create a reliable system capable of sorting fruits by size while maintaining speed and precision. I conducted independent research on existing mechanisms, analyzed different concepts, and selected the most efficient approach for the workspace requirements.

Using CATIA and SolidWorks, I created the full 3D model of the machine, including the conveyor, guiding system, and sorting mechanism. I iterated the design based on feasibility and manufacturability, then validated the dimensions and mechanical behavior. I also collaborated with more experienced team members when necessary to ensure the design aligned with real production constraints.

The final result was a functional, manufacturable design that demonstrated my ability to work independently, solve problems creatively, and deliver a complete engineering solution from concept to final model.
